Jeff Kaplan

Overwatch finally dies after Jeff's departure.

Here's a time line of Jeff Kaplan's carrer at Blizzard:

  • 2002 - Kaplan applied and was hired into Blizzard by May 2002, he was helping with quality assurance for Warcraft III: Reign of Chaos.
  • 2007 - Worked as designer for World of Warcraft: The Burning Crusade.
  • 2008 - Worked as designer for World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King.
  • 2009 - Jeff steped down as game director for WoW to switch his role at Blizzard to a new, unannounced MMO, Titan.
  • 2014 - Titan was considered an ambitious project and it was canceled by that year.
  • 2016 - Jeff and Metzen(co-worker) led this team to take some of the gameplay and creative elements of Titan and created a team-based shooter, which became Overwatch.
  • 2021 - Blizzard announced that Jeff was leaving the company after nineteen years on April 20th.
